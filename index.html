Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Kosify Owner ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information obtained through the Kosify Owner Android application (the "Application"). Kosify Owner is a property and boarding house (kos) management platform that enables property owners to manage tenants, view statistics, process payments, and withdraw earnings.

1. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Application, including property owners and users who interact with payment and communication features. By accessing or using the Application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal and Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored securely using hashing and encryption)
  • Profile information

b. Financial and Transaction Information

  • Bank account number and account holder name (for withdrawal and payout)
  • Transaction records, payment history, and withdrawal history
  • Billing and payment status

c. Communication and User Content

  • Chat messages between users and the platform
  • Photos, images, or documents uploaded to the Application

d. Location Information

  • Approximate or precise location to support property listing, statistics, and location-based features

e. Device, Log, and Usage Data

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system and application version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, and usage statistics

f. Statistical and Analytical Data

  • Occupancy and revenue statistics
  • Payment trends and usage analytics
  • Aggregated and anonymized performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, authenticate, and manage user accounts
  • To operate core management features for kos owners
  • To process payments, withdrawals, and financial reconciliation
  • To generate statistical reports and analytics
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To improve application functionality and performance
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • To comply with applicable laws and financial regulations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (Where Applicable)

We process personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds: user consent, contractual necessity, compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate business interests related to operating and improving the Application.

5. Payment Processing

Payment transactions within the Application may be processed through trusted third-party payment service providers. We do not store complete payment card information. Financial data is handled in accordance with applicable payment security standards.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With payment gateways, banks, or financial partners to process transactions and withdrawals
  • With service providers that support hosting, analytics, and infrastructure
  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • To protect the rights, safety, and property of users or the public

7. Data Retention

We retain personal and financial information only for as long as necessary to fulfill operational, contractual, and legal obligations, including accounting and regulatory requirements.

8. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational security measures to protect personal data, including encryption, access controls, and regular monitoring. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

9. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Request information about how your data is processed

10. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting us using the details below. Certain data may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as financial record-keeping.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be published on this page, and continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
